Discover Our Program
Our Concept

Making Learning Fun, Practical, and Community-Driven

Tutor-Kain is a community program that offers free tutorials in essential subjects while providing simple, wholesome meals and a motivating reward system.

Our goal is to create a positive feedback loop where education and nutrition support each other, empowering students to succeed and building a stronger, more connected community.

How It Works

Our Three Pillars of Support

Study Meal Share

Simple, nutritious food like bread, porridge, or biscuits are provided during sessions to ensure students are full and focused while studying.

What We Teach

Our volunteer tutors cover essential subjects including Basic Math, Practical English, and fundamental Digital Skills to prepare them for the future.

Learning Coupons

Students earn coupons for attendance and participation, which can be redeemed for school supplies, snacks, or local store discounts, motivating them to keep learning.
